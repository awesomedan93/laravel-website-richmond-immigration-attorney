EXPERIENCED CRIMINAL DEFENSE LAWYERS FIGHTING FOR YOU
Raul Novo and Soulmaz Taghavi are experienced criminal defense lawyers serving the Richmond, Henrico, Chesterfield and surrounding areas. ​

Raul Novo is a savvy and passionate lawyer who has, for over twenty years, dedicated himself to criminal defense cases. Raul has fought for his clients in both federal and local courts throughout Central Virginia. Raul has represented clients facing drug charges, DUI arrests and other criminal violations of the law in both bench and jury trials. ​

Soulmaz Taghavi is an experienced lawyer who specializes in criminal defense and immigration law. Soulmaz honed her skills as a criminal defense lawyer as a legal intern in the Durham District Attorney’s Office of the 14th District and later as a clerk at the Superior Court of the District of Columbia in Washington, D. C. Soulmaz has successfully handled a variety of criminal and immigration matters in her legal career in both federal and local courts. Soulmaz is multilingual and is fluent in Persian, Dari and proficient in Spanish. ​
